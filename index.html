Biography

I am pursuing my Ph.D. at Beihang University, supervised by Prof. Danpei Zhao. I received the B.S. and M.S. degrees from Beihang University in 2019 and 2022, respectively. From 2020 to 2021, I worked as a Research Intern at ByteDance AI Lab.

My research lies at the intersection of machine learning, computer vision, and remote sensing, with a particular focus on continual learning, panoptic perception, and multimodal scene understanding.

I have authored papers in IEEE TPAMI, IEEE TIP, IEEE TGRS, ACM MM, etc.

Research Interests

  • Continual learning and lifelong visual understanding
  • Multimodal learning for remote sensing images
  • Trustworthy AI models
